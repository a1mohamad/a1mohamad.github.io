Project Conclusion

Final insights and summary of the 2024 California Housing research.

The Linear Regression model served as a robust baseline for this housing price prediction task. Achieving an MSE of 0.548 and an MAE of 0.538, the model effectively captured the primary trends within the 20,640 records.

Key Insight: The residual analysis indicates that while the model is highly accurate for mid-range properties, future work should involve feature engineering for geographical density to address higher-variance predictions in luxury markets.
